Projects

Material Model Assessment

I am currently conducting research on the application of the variational principle for assessing material models, with a focus on estimating the elastic stiffness of IM7 graphite composites. This study aims to deepen the understanding of their elastic properties by exploring the variational approach as a third method, complementing earlier studies that used the divergence theorem and regression analysis. A key aspect of the research involves investigating the presence of asymmetry in the compliance matrix, as reported in prior work.

Common Effluent Treatment Plant (CETP) Project

As a Research Assistant in the collaborative project titled "Feasibility Study of Common Effluent Treatment Plant in Birgunj-Patlaiya Sector", conducted with the Ministry of Industry, Commerce, and Supplies, Government of Nepal, I contributed to the assessment of industrial waste management strategies through data analytics and technical documentation. My responsibilities included assisting in data collection and analysis, as well as supporting project management efforts to ensure a thorough evaluation of the region’s treatment needs. I also collaborated closely with civil and environmental engineers in layout planning using AutoCAD and MATLAB, helping to translate technical findings into practical design solutions for the proposed CETP.

Optimization of Penstock Diamerter

For the Middle Kali Gandaki Hydropower Project in Nepal, I contributed to the optimization of penstock diameter using graphical methods in MATLAB and Excel. The focus was on identifying the most cost-effective diameter by analyzing the relationship between construction costs and hydraulic energy losses. By applying principles of fluid mechanics and pipe flow modeling, we determined the economic diameter that minimized the total project cost. To support the analysis, I also created detailed CAD drawings in AutoCAD to represent various penstock configurations and aid in visual evaluation of the design alternatives.

Screw Turbine Performance Analysis

As part of a study titled Screw Turbine Performance Analysis: CFD Simulation with Changing Design Parameters, I performed computational fluid dynamics (CFD) simulations in ANSYS Fluent to evaluate the efficiency and flow behavior of a screw turbine under varying design conditions. By leveraging meshing and parametric tools, I assessed the accuracy of different turbine configurations and enhanced the predictive capability of the model. The analysis provided valuable insights into how design modifications influence performance, contributing to the optimization of turbine geometry for improved efficiency in sustainable energy applications.

Multi-Directional Wind Turbine Optimization

I led a 4-member team in the design and simulation of Savonius-Darrieus hybrid wind turbines for a study titled Multi-Directional Wind Turbine with Combined Savonius and Darrieus Rotors: A Comparative Performance Analysis. Using SolidWorks for detailed modeling and ANSYS for advanced CFD simulations, we evaluated the performance of various multi-directional turbine configurations, focusing on their efficiency in capturing wind energy from different directions. The study involved analyzing fluid-structure interactions and energy conversion processes to identify optimal design features. Our findings were presented at a research showcase, and we compiled a comprehensive technical report detailing the design methodology, simulation results, and performance comparisons.

Finite Element Analysis of UAV Structural Integrity

In a project focused on Finite Element Analysis, I assessed the stress distribution in UAV structures designed by various companies using OptiStruct as the solver and HyperMesh for preprocessing and postprocessing. By analyzing structural responses under different loading conditions, I identified critical stress regions and contributed to design modifications that enhanced the overall safety and performance of the UAVs. The insights gained from this analysis supported more robust and reliable structural designs.
